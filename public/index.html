Welcome

This is the website for the upcoming mobile app called the "PRiSM Perception App". The PRiSM Perception App is a generic tool that can be configured for different concerts, presentations and experiments. The data gathered will be analysed to explore what mathematics the listener may be hearing.

The Music of Proof

The upcoming performance "The Music Of Proof: What Does Maths Sound Like?" - more details here - will use a mobile app to give feedback on a performance taking place on the night of the 4th October 2017.

Instructions

This mobile app should be available in mid September and run on both Android and iOS devices. Check back here later for instructions on getting hold of this app.
