Welcome

This is the website for the upcoming mobile app called the "PRiSM Perception App". The PRiSM Perception App will be a generic tool that can be configured for different concerts, presentations and experiments. It will gather data which will be analysed to explore what mathematical relationships the listener may be hearing.

Performance: Music and Maths

The upcoming performance "Music and Maths" - more detail here - will use a mobile app to give feedback on a musical piece taking place on the evening of the 27 January 2018.
